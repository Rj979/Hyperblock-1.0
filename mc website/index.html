Server Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members. Any form of hate speech or harassment will not be tolerated.
  • Do not use any mods or hacks that give you an unfair advantage over other players. This includes x-ray, fly hacks, and auto-clickers.
  • Griefing, stealing, and raiding other players' bases is strictly prohibited. This includes destroying or modifying another player's builds without their consent.
  • Do not spam the chat or use excessive caps, emojis, or symbols.
  • Do not advertise other servers or websites without permission from a staff member.
  • Any attempt to exploit glitches or bugs in the server to gain an advantage is not allowed.
  • Do not impersonate other players or staff members.
  • English is the main language on the server. Please speak in English in the global chat.
  • Violation of any of these rules may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban at the discretion of the staff team.
